Some words about us

Where Great Food Meets Memorable Celebrations

At Onion Leaf – Restaurant and Party Center, we create a perfect blend of great food, elegant ambiance, and warm hospitality. Located in Dhaka, our restaurant offers a variety of Thai, Chinese, and Bangla cuisine, prepared with fresh ingredients to ensure a delightful dining experience for every guest.

We also specialize in hosting memorable events, including weddings, birthdays, and corporate programs. With a well-designed venue and dedicated service team, we ensure every occasion is smoothly organized and truly unforgettable.
